Knockdown Results

2⁻ΔΔCt Method (Livak & Schmittgen, 2001)

Step 1 – Average technical duplicates (2 wells → 1 bio-rep):
mean_Ct = (Ct_rep1 + Ct_rep2) / 2
Step 2 – ΔCt per bio-rep:
ΔCt = mean_Ct(target) − mean_Ct(reference gene)
Step 3 – NTC baseline ΔCt (pool all NTC bio-reps per primer):
mean_ΔCt_NTC = mean of all NTC ΔCt values for that primer
Step 4 – ΔΔCt:
ΔΔCt = ΔCt(siRNA) − mean_ΔCt_NTC
Step 5 – Relative expression & KD%:
Rel. Exp. = 2^(−ΔΔCt)
KD% = (1 − Rel. Exp.) × 100

Mean ± SE reported across biological replicates (NTC-mix1/2/3/4).
